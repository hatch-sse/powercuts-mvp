About this data

Data is based on captured SSEN power interruption records and is designed for internal insight and campaign planning.

Mapping is at postcode sector level, not full-postcode boundary level. Only sectors within SSEN SHEPD/SEPD licence areas are included.

Total time off supply is approximate and based on captured outage windows. Meta Ads targeting exports use postcode sectors because that is the practical targeting geography supported by the workflow.
